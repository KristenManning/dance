Classes

We offer several styles of dance at Barbara's School of the Dance. From Monday through Friday, we offer 1 hour long classes in the following styles: Ballet, Tap, and Jazz.

For those who have danced many years and have been approved to go up on Pointe, there are classes available for you.

For the inexperienced and younger dancer, we offer combo classes. The two types of combo classes are ballet/jazz and tap/jazz. These classes are designed to introduce young children to two types of dance during 1 hour of instruction.
